Community Guidelines

Star Connect Hub thrives when everyone treats each other with dignity and respect. These guidelines help us maintain a premium, safe, and joyful platform for fans and creators alike.

Our Values

Star Connect Hub is a premium marketplace where fans connect with their favourite creators for personalised, meaningful moments. Our community is built on three core values:

✦

Respect

Every interaction — fan to creator, creator to fan — should be respectful, warm, and professional.

✦

Authenticity

We verify every creator. Fans deserve to know they're connecting with the real person.

✦

Safety

A safe platform for everyone, free from harassment, exploitation, and harm.

Remember: Our community guidelines exist to protect you. When in doubt, ask yourself — "Would I be comfortable if Star Connect Hub could see exactly what I'm doing?" If yes, proceed. If not, reconsider.

Fan Conduct

As a fan booking experiences on Star Connect Hub, we ask you to:

Do

  • Be clear and kind in your booking requests — creators put real effort into every personalised session.
  • Respect creator schedules — allow the advertised delivery time before raising a concern.
  • Leave honest, fair feedback — your ratings help others make informed decisions.
  • Contact support if something isn't right — we're here to help resolve issues fairly.

Don't

  • Send inappropriate, sexual, or threatening messages to creators or their teams.
  • Request content that violates our Content Rules (see below).
  • Attempt to solicit the creator's personal contact details or arrange off-platform transactions.
  • Share personalised content publicly without the creator's written consent.
  • Submit false or misleading booking information to manipulate content delivery.
  • Open fraudulent disputes or initiate bad-faith chargebacks.

Creator Standards

Verified creators represent the Star Connect Hub brand. We hold our creators to a high standard to ensure every fan interaction is exceptional.

Professionalism

  • Deliver personalised sessions within your stated timeframe. Consistent late deliveries may result in account review.
  • Communicate promptly — respond to fan messages within 48 hours.
  • If you cannot fulfil a booking due to personal circumstances, contact our team immediately to facilitate a refund.

Authenticity

  • Your profile must represent you honestly — accurate name, photos, and stated social reach.
  • You may not outsource the production of personalised content to another person without fan consent.
  • Do not create fake reviews, ratings, or testimonials.

Pricing

  • Your listed prices must be accurate and inclusive of any fees you intend to charge through the Platform.
  • You may update your pricing at any time; changes apply to new bookings only, not existing ones.
Verified Creator badge: Your gold verification badge is a mark of trust. Maintaining it requires upholding these standards. We periodically review creator accounts — sustained violations can result in badge removal.

Content Rules

All content created or shared through Star Connect Hub must meet the following standards:

Allowed content

  • Personalised video messages, shoutouts, and greetings
  • Q&A sessions, advice, and motivational content
  • Behind-the-scenes content and exclusive updates
  • Music performances, readings, and creative content
  • Business coaching, mentorship, and professional guidance from qualified creators
  • Content that promotes positivity, inspiration, and genuine fan connection

Content that requires care

  • Political or sensitive topics: Creators may address these but should do so responsibly and without inciting hatred.
  • Health or medical advice: Always accompanied by a disclaimer recommending professional consultation.
  • Fan-submitted personal information: Handle with discretion and do not reference it beyond the requested content.

What's Not Allowed

The following content and behaviour is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action:

Zero-tolerance violations — these result in immediate, permanent account termination without appeal:
  • Content that sexually exploits minors in any way
  • Content that incites violence against specific individuals or groups
  • Selling or facilitating illegal substances, weapons, or services
  • Impersonation of another real person's identity

Harassment & bullying

  • Targeted harassment, threats, or abuse directed at any user
  • Coordinated attacks or pile-ons against a creator or fan
  • Repeated unwanted contact after being asked to stop
  • Doxxing — sharing someone's private information without consent

Spam & manipulation

  • Sending unsolicited promotional messages to other users
  • Fake reviews, incentivised ratings, or review manipulation
  • Creating duplicate accounts after suspension
  • Using bots or automation to interact with the Platform

Misleading content

  • False claims about your identity, credentials, or professional status
  • Booking content under false pretences to obtain specific statements
  • Spreading demonstrably false information that could harm others

Financial misconduct

  • Money laundering or using the Platform for illicit financial transactions
  • Fraudulent payment claims or bad-faith chargebacks
  • Off-platform solicitation to bypass our escrow and fee system

Authenticity & Verification

Trust is the foundation of our platform. Every creator on Star Connect Hub has undergone identity verification. Here's what that means for you:

  • Verified Badge (✓): This blue badge on a creator's profile means we have verified their real-world identity matches their listed professional credentials.
  • No impersonators: If you believe a profile is impersonating someone, report it immediately. We take these reports seriously and act within 24 hours.
  • Authentic interactions only: All platform interactions must be genuine. We do not permit scripted or AI-generated personalised responses presented as authentic.

Privacy & Safety

  • Protect personal data: Do not request, share, or collect other users' personal information beyond what is necessary for your booking.
  • No covert recording: Do not record live sessions without consent.
  • Respect boundaries: If a creator or fan indicates they are uncomfortable with a request, respect that boundary immediately.
  • Safeguard your account: Never share your login credentials. Star Connect Hub staff will never ask for your password.
  • Report suspicious activity: If you notice unusual activity on your account or receive suspicious messages, contact us immediately.

Reporting Violations

If you witness a violation of these guidelines, please report it. Your reports help keep the community safe.

How to report

  • Email: starconnecthub@gmail.com with the subject line "Report: [type of issue]"
  • Include: the username or profile link, a description of the violation, and any supporting screenshots.

What happens after you report

  • We acknowledge all reports within 24 hours.
  • Our trust and safety team reviews each report within 3 business days.
  • We keep reports confidential — the reported person will not know who filed the report.
  • We share the outcome with you once the review is complete.
False reports: Filing a report in bad faith — to harm a creator or fan without legitimate cause — is itself a violation of these guidelines and may result in your account being suspended.

Enforcement

We apply a progressive enforcement model for most violations. However, severe violations (see "Zero-tolerance" above) result in immediate permanent termination.

1

Warning

A formal notice explaining the violation and the expected change in behaviour. The account remains active.

2

Temporary Suspension

Account access restricted for 7 to 30 days. Pending bookings are honoured or refunded. Earnings are held pending review.

3

Permanent Termination

Irreversible account closure. Forfeiture of pending earnings where the violation caused harm to another user. Referral to law enforcement where required.

Appeals

If you believe your account was actioned in error, you may appeal within 14 days by emailing starconnecthub@gmail.com with subject "Appeal: [your username]". Our team will review your appeal within 7 business days. Zero-tolerance violations are not eligible for appeal.
